Hello and welcome to my professional website!

My name is Alexis Smith. I am a 28 year old educator living and working in the Central Arkansas area. I earned a bachelor’s degree in English language and literature in 2015, and am currently pursuing a master’s degree in Learning Technologies. I earned my educators license in 2016 and have been teaching in a secondary-level environment for the past 4 years; I’ve taught English I for 3 years and am currently teaching English III. Recently, I have been appointed the head coach for my school’s esports team, and have started leading training and professional development sessions for my colleagues. Along the way I have earned certifications such as:

  • The Microsoft Innovative Educator certification
  • Level 1 & 2 Google Certified Educator
  • Flipgrid Certified Educator
  • Advancement Via Individual Determination (AVID) core teacher certification

I pride myself on being a quick learner, a hard worker, tenacious, and easily adaptable.
